Design

In this project i have used all general tags like buttom,iframe,img,div,header,footer.I have tried to enhance the quality and appearance by using visual components like opacity and shadows in CSS. I played with font family,font weight,font size and used icons in footer section.For navigation i used a satndard navbar and gave the links a hover effect.The major special effects used by me are border-shadow, opacity,hover,flex,image in background box and opacity

Introduction

This is a personalized website made for a GYM and targets the audience interested in health,fitness and gym.The website include 5 pages(home,about us , memberships ,contact us and about developer).The membership page include fitness plans to the viewer along with a contact page to convieniently seek support from the gym . A map location(which is fake) has also been added to feature the iframe tag.The design elements include color grading , border shadow and hover effects.Also,features like opacity, placeholders and flexbox has been used.
